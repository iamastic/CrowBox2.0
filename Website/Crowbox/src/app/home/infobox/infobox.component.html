Training Stage 1 During the first training stage, all functions of the CrowBox are disabled. Crows may interact with the box however they see fit.
The lid to the food is opened up.
The box acts like a bird feeder.
The goal is to acclimatise the birds to the box's look, feel and sounds.
Training Stage 2 In the Second Training stage, the aim is to encourage crows to land on top of the CrowBox. The roof of the box sits on a couple of switches which once pressed, open the Food Lid. This is the first step in training the crows to follow human instructions.
The lid to the food is closed.
Crows will press a switch by landing on the roof.
Pressing the switch results in the Food Lid sliding open for the crows to feed on.

In Stage 3, the crows will have access to a set of coins which they must figure out how to deposit into the box. Upon successfully depositing a coin down the access point, the food lid will open and they will once again be rewarded.
The 4th and Final Stage functions similar to Stage 3, except the coins will not be provided to the crows as they will be expected to find and bring their own from the environment.
